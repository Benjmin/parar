Info

item: pARA-R
length: 702 bp
This plasmid is genetically engineered to allow scientist to produce a mutant Red Flourescent Protein. It contains control elements as well and is the subject of the model.

item: rfp
length: 702
This gene is called Red Flourescent Protein(rfp). Rfp is found in Discosoma sp., a sea urchin. This gene is what we as a class are experimenting with and is extracted using BamH I and Hind III.

item: ampr
length: ?
This gene is called the ampicillin resistance gene(ampr). It provides the bacteria with resistance against penicillin and is one of the control elements in the pARA-R plasmid.
